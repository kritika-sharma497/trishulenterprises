ABOUT US

TRISHUL ENTERPRISES in New Delhi was established in 1999 with an aim for executing in and Installation & Commissioning :- Out of contracts regarding Electrification including Sub-Station, DG sets and Distribution Systems. Having vast experience gained over the past Years, we provide turnkey solutions resulting into an exulted customers.
We are registered Contractor with the Govt. of Delhi NCT, MES, PWD and with Govt. of India for Electrical work and Maintenance. Our Firm is registered with TIN, Service Tax, ESIC & EPF Departments.
